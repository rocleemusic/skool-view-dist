# skool-view — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-30_

skool-view is a browser extension that provides an alternative, faster interface for Skool.com
communities you already belong to. It runs entirely in your own browser using your own logged-in
Skool session. There is no skool-view server.

## What it accesses

- **Your existing skool.com session cookie** — so it can read and post as you, exactly as the
  normal Skool site does.
- **Page content on skool.com** while the overlay is active.
- **Local browser storage** — to remember your preferences (theme, whether the overlay is on,
  locally-pinned posts, list width, and zoom).

## What it does with it

Everything happens locally in your browser. The extension makes network requests **only to
skool.com** — the same servers the normal site uses. Your data is never sent to the developer or
to any third party.

## What it does NOT do

- It does not send your data to the developer or any third party.
- It has no analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
- It runs on **no site other than skool.com**.

## Retention

Preferences are stored locally on your device and are removed when you uninstall the extension.
skool-view retains no data about you anywhere else.
